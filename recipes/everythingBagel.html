Everything Bagel

Description

A perfect breakfast snack that will get you started for the day ahead.

What you'll need:

  • your favorite bagel brand
  • your favorite plain cream cheese
  • everything Bagel Seasoning
  • a toaster

Let's make it:

  1. toast your bagel to desired crispyness.
  2. with a butter knife, spread your cream cheese evenly on both peices.
  3. sprinkle a light amount of Everything Bagel Seasoning on both peices.
  4. put peices together (or not) and enjoy!
