Caprese Salad

Description

a crisp and fresh summer snack perfect for an evening get together.

What you'll need:

  • 2 to 4 large heirloom tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 8 to 12 basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

    • For the Balsamic Glaze:

  • 1 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 Tbs of sugar

Now lets put it all together:

  1. Cut the tomatoes into medium to thin slices.
  2. cut the fresh mozzarella into slices of the same width.
  3. pull off the best looking 8 to 12 basil leaves
  4. lay the mozzarella on top of the tomatoes, garnish with basil and salt and pepper.
  5. Finally, drizzle with olive oil.

    6. Now on to the Balsamic Glaze:

    1. simmer the cup of balsamic vinegar on medium along with the sugar for 5 to 10 minutes. Make sure not to leave it unattended and stir occasionally because it may scorch.
    2. Once cooled, drizzle Balsamic glaze on top of the other ingredients and Enjoy!
